Dr. Peter Mena

Diagnostic & Interventional Radiology

Board-certified in both diagnostic and interventional radiology, Dr. Peter Mena provides comprehensive radiology services ranging from complex cross-sectional imaging interpretation to minimally invasive vascular and non-vascular interventional procedures.

With training at UT Southwestern in interventional radiology and Richmond University Medical Center in diagnostic radiology, he brings reliable, evidence-based practice to every assignment. Available for locum tenens engagements nationwide.

Dr. Jessica Mena

Anesthesiology & Aesthetics

Board-certified anesthesiologist with a passion for both perioperative medicine and aesthetic treatments. Dr. Jessica Mena trained at New York Medical College, completed her internal medicine internship at Greenwich Hospital in Greenwich, CT, and her anesthesiology residency at University Hospital in Newark, NJ.

In addition to providing expert anesthesia services, Dr. Mena offers aesthetic treatments including Botox and dermal fillers, helping patients look and feel their best with a physician's trained eye and meticulous technique.
